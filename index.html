Tube User Guide

Step 1:Register an addr pair to the Tube Send 0.777 NBT and 0.00777 BTC to the Tube FROM a NBT address and a BTC address of yours, then the two send-from addresses will be registered.
Step 2:Check live addr pairs Check Live Addr Pairs to see if your addr pair is successfully registered.
Step 3: Exchange coins by using the Tube Send NBT/BTC to the Tube (the minimun is 100 NBT or 0.2 BTC) FROM NBT/BTC side of your addr pair, then you will receive BTC/NBT by another side of the addr pair shortly.
