Tube User Guide ( Step 0 Know how to send from an address )

Step 1Register an addr pair to the Tube Just simply Fill a Register Form ; Or send ... NBT and ... BTC to the Tube FROM a NBT address and a BTC address of yours, then the two send-from addresses will be registered.
